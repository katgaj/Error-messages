Confirmation

A confirmation message is a response or feedback provided to a user to acknowledge that their action has been successfully completed. It confirms that the system has received and processed the user's input or request as intended.

Confirmation messages are commonly used in various contexts, such as:

  • form submissions: after a user fills out and submits a form on a website or application, a confirmation message confirms that the form has been successfully submitted.

  • transactions: in e-commerce or banking applications, a confirmation message is displayed after a purchase or financial transaction to confirm that the transaction has been processed.

  • account actions: when users perform actions such as creating an account, updating profile information, or changing settings, a confirmation message confirms that the action has been completed.

  • deletions or modifications: when users delete or modify content, such as files, messages, or settings, a confirmation message ensures that they are aware of the action and confirms their decision.

Confirmation messages provide reassurance, reduce uncertainty, and prevent errors or misunderstandings.
